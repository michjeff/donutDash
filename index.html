about

Carnegie Mellon Univeristy's Donut Dash is an epic 2 mile mind-over-stomach race around the perimeter of CMU campus. Donut Dash has raised over $30,000 ALL of which benefits the Children's Hospital of Pittsburgh of UPMC. This year, Donut dash will be bigger and better then ever.

Runners take a 2 mile path around CMU campus during which they will stop briefly to consume their donuts. We also have leisure division if you care to walk and even a short kids course for child friendly Donut Dashing!

Whether you're competitive and slurp down a water slogged 2400 calories of donuts or prefer a leisurely meal with fork and knife we will ensure you are provided your daily nutritional value of donuts (← that's a thing, right?)
